“Organizations, by their very nature are designed to promote order and routine. They are inhospitable environments for innovation.” (T. Levitt).

SteamFab offers a unique environment

We share our co-working environment with DAT Venture, an early-stage accelerator for interantional startups. It is attractive and full of passionate and entrepreneurial people. A truly creative environment where innovation is happening.

Keep-up with the entrepreneur generation

There is an old way and a new way. The new way is lean, agile, and based on experimentation. It is much faster than the old way. The new way can be learned. Often it is a matter of processes and tools - and a significant change in culture.

Going outside the box requires a mentor

Established companies are full of innovation and smart people. What is often lacking is the space to go outside the box. A mentor and a new environment is what we provide.

We optimize your risk and reward

The SteamFab environment is ideally suited to foster innovation. Your team gets immersed with the millenial generation of entrepreneurs. You participate in our curriculum and learn how to translate ideas into traction.

Individually tailored program

We are flexible and offer an imersion program that is just right for you. Our curriculum is available on-demand and we adapt it to your needs. Duration is also flexible, but we suggest a minimum of three months. During that time a significant milestone towards a new product should be reached.

Innovation is as much a function of the environment as it is a matter of skill. Rapidly inventing and building digital solutions can be learned. The tools we use are constantly evolving. You have to learn not to trust your gut and instead talk to your users, experiment, and iterate. Getting to scale requires excellent metrics. We know how to do it and we will teach you.
